Chicken Shawarma

Description

Inspired by the recipe shared by Ben Stiller and Ahmed Badr on Tasty, this recipe modifies several elements for a dairy-free chicken shawarma that is packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1.1 kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs, trimmed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon sumac
  • 1/4 teaspoon mild chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced

Steps

  1. Process chicken, leaving chicken thighs whole.
  2. Mix all spices, salt, garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice to make marinade.
  3. Marinate for at least 2 hours (up to 24 hours).
  4. On a single layer in a preheated oven (180C with fan), cook marinated chicken for 40-50 minutes. Check temperature with probe thermometer.
  5. After resting, slice into 2-3 cm strips.
